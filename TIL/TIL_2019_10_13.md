### insertAdjacentElement
- document.insertAdjacentElement("위치",element);
- 위치 종류
    - "afterbegin" 
    - 첫번째 자식
    - After the beginning of the element (as the first child)

    - "afterend" 
    - 바로 앞
    - After the element
    - "beforebegin"
    - 바로 뒤
    -  Before the element
    - "beforeend" 
    - 마지막 자식
    -  Before the end of the element (as the last child)
